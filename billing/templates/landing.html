About This Project

Description: Working in a tech company whose mandate is mainly internet service provision, we experienced many problems in the management of client subscriptions, payments, and control of their internet services. This led to the development of an application that manages all these tasks in one place, allowing our team to work more efficiently and our clients to enjoy a better service experience.

Key Features: The application allows for easy management of subscriptions, internet service control, and seamless integration with payment systems like MPESA, ensuring a holistic approach to ISP management.
